About

Math 4 Kids is a simple and fun interactive mobile web site kids can interact with to teach themselves Math and Math related concepts. Knowing and understanding Math is needed throughout life helping kids in there early years will give them a jump start at sucess.

This site also includes addition, subtraction, mutiplication and division calculators to help kids teach themselves math. Soon interactive equations will be added to each section to make learining more challenging and fun.
