About

Business Landing is a web site and moible app that allows enterpenures, small, and large business to decide where to locate or plant there business based on location, demorgraphics, citizen income, and much more. At this time Business Landing only utlizes census data and google maps to select the best locaton for entrepenures and corporations who want to start businesses.
